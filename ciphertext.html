Cipher Tips

  • Use a phrase key for Vigenere; avoid numbers and symbols.
  • Caesar shifts move letters by a fixed amount; try 3 for classic text.
  • Rail Fence uses zig-zag rows; the depth controls the rails.
  • XOR works on bytes; keep the key short but not empty.
  • AES requires a passphrase and is best for encrypted data only.
